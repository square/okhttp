DeferredSocketAdapter

class DeferredSocketAdapter(socketAdapterFactory: DeferredSocketAdapter.Factory) : SocketAdapter

Deferred implementation of SocketAdapter that works by observing the socket and initializing on first use.

We use this because eager classpath checks cause confusion and excessive logging in Android, and we can't rely on classnames after proguard, so are probably best served by falling through to a situation of trying our least likely noisiest options.
