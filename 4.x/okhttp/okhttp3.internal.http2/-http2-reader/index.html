Http2Reader

class Http2Reader(source: BufferedSource, client: Boolean) : Closeable

Reads HTTP/2 transport frames.

This implementation assumes we do not send an increased frame to the peer. Hence, we expect all frames to have a max length of Http2.INITIAL_MAX_FRAME_SIZE.
